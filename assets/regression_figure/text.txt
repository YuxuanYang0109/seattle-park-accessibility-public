In addition, residential population data may underestimate demand generated by tourists, commuters, and other daytime users in the city center.'

Integrating LLM-based review analysis with Google Reviews and the 2SFCA method provides an activity-oriented accessibility framework. Ultimately, park equity concerns not only whether residents can reach a park, but whether they can access diverse, appropriate, and demand-responsive recreational opportunities.'
